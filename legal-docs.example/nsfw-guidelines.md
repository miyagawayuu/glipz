## NSFW Posting Guidelines

Edit this file to update the body shown on `/legal/nsfw-guidelines`.

### Required Conditions

- Mark posts as NSFW when they contain NSFW content.
- Avoid exposing sensitive content unexpectedly in thumbnails or list views.
- Only post works that do not infringe the rights of third parties.
- Follow the instance's age and consent requirements before posting, viewing, or
  sharing restricted content.

### Prohibited Content

- Content that violates applicable law
- Sexualized content involving real people or photo-based models
- Non-consensual sexual expression, threats, or harassment
- Content the operator determines to be dangerous or inappropriate

### Moderation

If a violation is confirmed, the operator may remove posts, change visibility,
restrict accounts, suspend accounts, limit federation with another domain, or
take other necessary actions.

### Federation

When federation is enabled, NSFW flags and related post metadata may be sent to
other Glipz-compatible servers. Remote servers may display, moderate, or retain
that metadata under their own policies.

### Operator Notes

This sample document replaces the built-in localized NSFW guidance when copied
into `LEGAL_DOCS_DIR`. Review the built-in app guidance and your local rules so
this Markdown file remains the authoritative policy for your instance.
