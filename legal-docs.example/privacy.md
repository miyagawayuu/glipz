## Privacy Policy

Edit this file to update the body shown on `/legal/privacy`.

### Information We Collect

- Registration information: email address, user ID, birth date or age-related
  confirmation fields, and information required for authentication
- Profile information: display name, bio, and images
- Post information: post text, images, videos, replies, reactions, and report details
- Direct message metadata and encrypted payloads: message timestamps,
  participants, encrypted message bodies, encrypted attachment metadata, and any
  plaintext a participant explicitly submits in a report
- Moderation information: post reports, federated post reports, DM reports,
  report categories, reasons, statuses, and operator review records
- Security and account information: two-factor authentication state, identity
  portability keys or encrypted transfer data, OAuth or provider connection
  metadata, and web push subscription records when those features are used
- Technical information: IP address, access timestamps, user agent, and
  security-relevant authentication events such as successful login or
  registration verification

### Purposes of Use

Collected information is used to provide the service, verify identity, maintain security, prevent abuse, respond to inquiries, and improve the service.

### Account Deletion, Retention, and Preservation

Users may request or use account deletion where the service provides it. Deletion
may be delayed or rejected while an active legal preservation hold applies to the
account or resource. Retention periods for backups, logs, media objects, and
moderation records should be defined by the operator's own policy and local
requirements.

### Law Enforcement and Preservation

The operator may preserve or disclose records when required by valid legal
process, emergency request, or preservation request. Direct message bodies and
attachments are client-side encrypted; the server can provide stored encrypted
payloads, metadata, user-submitted report plaintext, access metadata, and audit
records within the verified request scope, but cannot decrypt protected content
it does not possess. Legal-request audit records in disclosure packages are
limited to the administrator actions recorded for that specific request.

### Federation

When federation is enabled, public profiles, public posts, reactions, polls,
NSFW flags, remote-follow metadata, DM public keys, signed event metadata, and
selected gated-media unlock or entitlement metadata may be sent to and stored on
other servers. Other instances may retain copies under their own policies.
