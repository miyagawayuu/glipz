## Privacy Policy

Edit this file to update the body shown on `/legal/privacy`.

### Information We Collect

- Registration information: email address, user ID, and information required for authentication
- Profile information: display name, bio, and images
- Post information: post text, images, videos, replies, reactions, and report details
- Direct message metadata and encrypted payloads: message timestamps, participants, encrypted message bodies, encrypted attachment metadata, and any plaintext a participant explicitly submits in a report
- Technical information: IP address, access timestamps, browser information, and activity logs

### Purposes of Use

Collected information is used to provide the service, verify identity, maintain security, prevent abuse, respond to inquiries, and improve the service.

### Law Enforcement and Preservation

The operator may preserve or disclose records when required by valid legal process, emergency request, or preservation request. Direct message bodies and attachments are client-side encrypted; the server can provide stored encrypted payloads and metadata, but cannot decrypt protected content it does not possess.

### Federation

Public posts, profiles, and other information required for federation may be sent to and stored on other servers.
